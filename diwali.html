About Diwali

Diwali, also known as Deepavali, is the Hindu festival of lights celebrated every year in autumn. It signifies the victory of light over darkness and good over evil.

Celebrations

People celebrate Diwali by lighting diyas (oil lamps), decorating their homes, exchanging gifts, and enjoying delicious sweets and snacks.
